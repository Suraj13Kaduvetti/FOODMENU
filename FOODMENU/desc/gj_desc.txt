Gulab Jamun:
Gulab Jamun is a popular Indian dessert where deep-fried dumplings made from khoya (reduced milk) are soaked in a fragrant sugar syrup. These golden brown orbs, delicately spiced with cardamom, offer a melt-in-the-mouth experience. The syrup imparts a sweet and floral aroma, making each bite a decadent treat. Whether served warm or at room temperature, Gulab Jamun is a festive indulgence that captures the essence of celebration and joy in Indian sweets.
