Butter Chicken:
Butter Chicken, or Murgh Makhani, is a classic North Indian dish that epitomizes indulgence. Tender chicken pieces are marinated, grilled, and then bathed in a velvety tomato-based curry enriched with butter and cream. The harmonious blend of spices, including fenugreek and garam masala, imparts a rich and flavorful profile to the dish. The succulent chicken, coated in the luscious sauce, creates a symphony of taste and texture. Often paired with naan or rice, Butter Chicken is a beloved dish that has found its way onto menus worldwide.
