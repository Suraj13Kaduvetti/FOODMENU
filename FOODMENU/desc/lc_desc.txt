Tandoori Lamb Chop:
Tandoori Lamb Chop is a succulent and flavorful dish where lamb chops are marinated in a blend of yogurt and aromatic spices, then cooked in a tandoor (clay oven). The result is tender, smoky lamb with a charred exterior and a burst of spices in every bite. This dish showcases the mastery of tandoori cooking, infusing the meat with a delightful blend of flavors. Served with mint chutney and naan, Tandoori Lamb Chop is a carnivorous delight that exemplifies the bold and robust nature of Indian cuisine.
