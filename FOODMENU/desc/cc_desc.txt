Caramel Custard:
Caramel Custard is a timeless dessert that combines silky smooth custard with a luscious caramel topping. The custard is made with a mixture of eggs, milk, sugar, and vanilla, creating a velvety texture that melts in the mouth. The caramelized sugar layer on top adds a delightful contrast of sweetness and a hint of bitterness. Served chilled and often adorned with a drizzle of caramel sauce or fruit, this elegant dessert is a perfect finale to any meal, showcasing the simple yet exquisite combination of creamy custard and caramelized sugar.
