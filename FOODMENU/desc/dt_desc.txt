Dal Tadka:
Dal Tadka is a quintessential Indian dish featuring yellow lentils tempered with a tantalizing mix of mustard seeds, cumin, garlic, and onions. The lentils are slow-cooked to perfection, absorbing the aromatic spices and creating a hearty, flavorful stew. This vegetarian dish, often served with rice or bread, showcases the art of tempering spices, adding a burst of aroma and taste. Dal Tadka is a wholesome and nutritious staple in Indian households, embodying simplicity and culinary finesse.
