Ice Cream:
Ice Cream is a frozen treat that comes in a myriad of flavors, textures, and swirls, offering a delightful sensory experience. Made with a base of milk, cream, and sugar, the possibilities for creativity are endless. From classic vanilla and chocolate to exotic flavors like pistachio or salted caramel, ice cream caters to diverse palates. Whether served in a cone or a bowl, this frozen delight provides a cooling and indulgent experience, making it a favorite for all ages, especially during hot weather or as a comforting dessert year-round.
