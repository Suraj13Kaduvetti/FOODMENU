Fish Curry:
Fish Curry is a delectable dish where fish is simmered in a spiced coconut or tomato-based gravy, creating a harmonious blend of flavors. The choice of spices varies across regions, but common ingredients include mustard seeds, turmeric, and curry leaves. The fish absorbs the rich, tangy sauce, creating a delightful culinary experience. Whether enjoyed with rice or bread, Fish Curry showcases the diversity of Indian coastal cuisine, offering a burst of flavors that range from mildly spiced to intensely aromatic.
