Rice Pudding:
Rice Pudding, also known as Kheer in Indian cuisine, is a creamy and comforting dessert made by slow-cooking rice with milk and sugar. Infused with aromatic spices like cardamom and garnished with nuts or raisins, this dish has a rich and velvety texture that warms the soul. Served warm or chilled, rice pudding is a versatile treat that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with various toppings. Its simplicity and nostalgic appeal make it a cherished dessert, often prepared to celebrate festivals, gatherings, or as a sweet conclusion to a hearty meal.
