Vegetable Biryani:
Vegetable Biryani is a fragrant and aromatic rice dish where basmati rice is layered with mixed vegetables and an array of spices. This one-pot wonder is slow-cooked to allow the flavors to meld, resulting in a symphony of tastes and textures. The vibrant colors and the tantalizing aroma make Vegetable Biryani a feast for the senses. Served with raita or a side of yogurt, this vegetarian biryani exemplifies the rich culinary heritage of India, offering a satisfying and wholesome dining experience.
