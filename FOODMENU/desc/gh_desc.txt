Gajar Ka Halwa:
Gajar Ka Halwa is a delightful Indian dessert made by slow-cooking grated carrots in ghee (clarified butter) and milk until they transform into a rich, caramelized concoction. Sweetened with sugar and garnished with nuts, this winter delicacy is a comforting and indulgent treat. The aromatic cardamom adds a warm touch to the dish, making it a popular choice during festive occasions. Served warm or cold, Gajar Ka Halwa epitomizes the art of transforming humble ingredients into a luscious and irresistible dessert.
