Palak Paneer:
Palak Paneer is a vibrant Indian dish where succulent cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) are simmered in a lush spinach gravy. The velvety spinach puree, spiced with aromatic herbs and garam masala, envelops the soft paneer, creating a harmonious blend of textures and flavors. This nutritious dish is not only visually appealing but also a delightful symphony of earthy spinach and creamy paneer, making it a staple in Indian cuisine.
